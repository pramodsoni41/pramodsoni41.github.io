Consultancy & Expert Services

Extensive consultancy experience (130+ projects) for government agencies, PSUs, MES, NBCC, NMCG, and industry across the following areas of expertise:

🌊 Hydrology & Bridge Hydrology Reports

  • Hydrological analyses and design flood estimation for river bridges, culverts, and road crossings across UP, Bihar, Rajasthan, and Himachal Pradesh
  • Catchment area delineation, flood frequency analysis, and discharge estimation for ungauged and gauged sites
  • SWAT-based hydrological modelling for basin-scale water availability and runoff studies (Amelia, Varuna, Sarda basins)
  • Surface runoff and drainage studies for urban and peri-urban areas (Gorakhpur, Prayagraj, Varanasi)
  • Morphology and river behaviour reports (Ganga, Yamuna, Prayagraj confluence zone)

📈 Hydraulic Design Studies

  • Hydraulic design of open channels, drains, RCC pipe drains, and box culverts (multiple projects across UP)
  • Design and vetting of intake wells, pumping mains, and rising mains for water supply schemes (6 MLD to 100+ km pipelines)
  • Transient (water hammer) analysis for long-distance pipelines and pumping systems
  • Aqueduct design (Arail, Meja) and inverted siphon design (Saryu Pariyojna, Gonda)
  • Flapper gate and flow control structure design (Assi Nala, Varanasi)
  • Bridge scour analysis and hydraulic design studies for river bridges (Azamgarh, Ganga region)
  • Dewatering system design for construction projects (Firozabad, multi-site)
  • Hydraulic lines and DPR preparation for MES, NBCC, and AIIMS projects

🌍 Dam Safety & Dam Break Studies

  • Dam safety assessments and structural review for multiple dams: Sirsi, Ghori, Sukhra, Adwa, and Modha dams
  • Dam break analysis using HEC-RAS for inundation mapping and emergency action planning
  • Flood routing studies and downstream hazard assessment for existing dam structures
  • Check dam hydraulic design and site feasibility studies

🌊 Physical & Numerical River Modelling

  • Physical hydraulic river model studies for river reach characterisation (Farukhabad reach, Sarda River)
  • HEC-RAS 1D/2D numerical modelling for flood inundation mapping, sediment transport, and water quality
  • River morphology and channel behaviour analysis for engineering interventions (NMCG, Prayagraj)
  • River channelization and embankment design for flood and erosion protection (Ghaghra, Sharda, Saryu, Rapti — 9 sites)

💧 Water & Wastewater Treatment Design

  • Sewage Treatment Plant (STP) process design and vetting across multiple capacities: 6 MLD, 20 MLD, 32 MLD, 33 MLD, 42 MLD, 50 MLD, and larger plants
  • Water Treatment Plant (WTP) design and review (Tapovan Intake Well & WTP, Aurangabad 20 MLD)
  • Sewer collection system design, proof-checking, and DPR vetting (Alwar, Varanasi, Lucknow, Kanpur, multiple cities)
  • Interception chambers, rising mains, and sewerage network design

🏭 Environmental, Biodiversity & Groundwater Studies

  • Biodiversity and ecological studies for industrial projects (Adani Green, coal mines — Kanchenmuda Nala, Sodepur ECL)
  • Scientific investigation of mine-wise water availability and community use potential (ECL, multiple mines)
  • Groundwater feasibility and aquifer characterisation studies
  • Leaching testing, subsidence assessment, ambient air quality monitoring for ash pond sites (13 sites, Obra)
  • Feasibility study for pond restoration at Azad Park, Allahabad; rainwater harvesting at IFFCO Phulpur Township

🏗 Technical Vetting & DPR Evaluation

  • Technical vetting of DPRs for hydrology, hydraulics, water supply, drainage, and sewerage across government and public sector projects
  • Proof checking of MEP drawings and Design Basis Reports for AIIMS West Campus (Pkg-I & II), NBCC Centurion Park, MES Hydraulic projects
  • Third-party inspection of piped water supplies (37 schemes, Rampur; multiple schemes under Jal Jeevan Mission)
  • Evaluation of EOI and DPR submissions for water resource projects
  • Annual inspection of Ganga Pollution Intervention (GPI) structures across Ganga basin states
