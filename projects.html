Consultancy & Expert Services

Consultancy undertaken for government agencies, public sector undertakings, and industry across the following areas of expertise:

🌊 River Management & Flood Protection

  • River channelization and embankment design for flood and erosion protection on Ghaghra, Sharda, Saryu, and Rapti rivers (9 sites across UP)
  • Annual inspection of Ganga Pollution Intervention (GPI) structures operating across Ganga basin states
  • Design of hydraulic structures including inverted siphon (Saryu Pariyojna, Gonda)

💧 Water Supply & Rural Hydrology

  • Third-party inspection of 37 under-construction piped water supply schemes in Rampur District (MSDP/PM Jan Vikas Program)
  • Vetting of Detailed Project Reports under Jal Jeevan Mission
  • Feasibility study for rainwater harvesting scheme at IFFCO Phulpur Township

🏭 Environmental Impact & Site Assessment

  • Leaching testing, subsidence studies, groundwater aquifer characterization, and ambient air quality monitoring (3 sites each)
  • Techno-economics feasibility study for ash pond filling — Obra Section 2 & 3, Stone Quarry, Near Circuit House (13 sites total)
  • Scientific investigation of mine-wise water availability and community use potential (ECL)

🌊 Lake & Groundwater Restoration

  • Feasibility study for pond restoration at Azad Park, Allahabad
  • Mapping of groundwater quality, depletion zones, and potential recharge structures in Bundelkhand region
