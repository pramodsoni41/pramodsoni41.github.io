About

I work in Hydraulics & Water Resources Engineering (HWRE) with interests in hydrological and hydraulic modelling, water quality assessment, river systems, climate change impacts, and decision support tools. My research spans atmospheric science, aerosol–climate interactions, remote sensing, GIS, and machine learning applications in environmental sciences.

Research Interests

  • Hydrological modelling (SWAT, HEC-RAS, WRF/WRF-Chem)
  • River basin planning & decision support systems
  • Water quality monitoring and treatment
  • Remote sensing, GIS & Google Earth Engine
  • Machine learning in environmental sciences
  • Aerosol–climate interactions & atmospheric science

Key Highlights

  • GIZ-funded Barak River Basin Management Plan (KWMI, DPSIR framework)
  • Smart Laboratory on Clean Rivers (SLCR) — Secretariat & DSS
  • Namami Gange projects totalling Rs. 19.75 Crores
  • Hydraulic design vetting & consultancy (CPHEEO/IS codes)
  • Best Poster Award, IITM Annual Monsoon Workshop, Pune (2015)
