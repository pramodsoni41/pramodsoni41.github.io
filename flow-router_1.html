Flow Router

Water pours from the source. Draw ramps by dragging on the canvas to route the flow into the turbine and fill it to 100%. You have a limited length of ramp material (the ink bar, bottom-left). Rougher channels slow the water; smoother ones let it race. Water that escapes off-screen hurts your efficiency score.

Drag to draw · ink bar shows ramp material left
E = erase mode · D = draw mode · C = clear · Space = pause flow
