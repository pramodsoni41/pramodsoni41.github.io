Entertainment

Just for Fun
🎮
Block Drop — Tetris Name Reveal
Type any name (A–Z, 0–9, up to 10 characters) and watch Tetris blocks fall to spell it out. Opens in a new window.
↗
💧
Watershed Water-Balance Simulator
Interactive rainfall–runoff game: drag the controls to split a storm into runoff, ET, soil storage and recharge (SCS Curve Number method), then take on the Design Challenge mode. Opens in a new window.
↗
🌊
Bernoulli Flow Lab
Fluid-mechanics game: push fluid through a venturi and watch continuity, Bernoulli's pressure drop, Reynolds number and cavitation come alive — with laminar/suction/cavitation Flow Challenges. Opens in a new window.
↗
⚫
Falling-Sphere Viscometer
Fluid-properties game: drop a sphere through viscous fluid and measure viscosity from its terminal velocity (Stokes' law) — explore density, specific gravity, buoyancy and the viscosity–temperature effect. Some spheres float! Opens in a new window.
↗
🌀
Cavitation Run
Arcade game (CE-211): drive the flow through a narrowing pipe and keep the throat pressure above the vapour pressure — squeeze too hard and cavitation destroys your pump. Opens in a new window.
↗
🔨
Water-Hammer Valve
Timing game (CE-211): close the valve fast for score, but feather it so the water-hammer surge (Joukowsky) stays below the pipe's burst rating. Opens in a new window.
↗
🧪
Newtonian or Not?
Sorting game (CE-211): read each fluid's shear-stress curve and classify it as Newtonian, non-Newtonian, ideal plastic or thixotropic. Opens in a new window.
↗
🔍
Guess the Fluid
Wordle-style deduction (CE-211): identify the mystery fluid in six tries from higher/lower clues on density, viscosity, surface tension and specific gravity. Opens in a new window.
↗
🫧
Droplet · Bubble · Jet Duel
Surface-tension puzzle (CE-211): predict which of a droplet (2σ/r), soap bubble (4σ/r) or jet (σ/r) holds the higher internal pressure. Opens in a new window.
↗
🧗
Capillary Climb
Capillarity puzzle (CE-211): tune the tube bore, liquid and contact angle so the meniscus hits the target line — water rises, mercury depresses (h = 2σcosθ/wr). Opens in a new window.
↗
🎮
Flow Router
Puzzle game: draw ramps to route falling water into the turbine and charge it to 100%, with limited ramp material and a Manning-style channel-roughness slider across five levels. Opens in a new window.
